Yahoo! Project

Notepad - Yahoo! With You for Life

The Problem
Yahoo was once the face of the internet. Now? It’s just your burner email.

The Insight
Everyone has an embarrassing first Yahoo email. And at some point, they’ve had to say it out loud.

The Big Idea
Yahoo… With You for Life. Instead of ignoring how people use Yahoo today, we lean in.

How It Works: Social First Movement
#MyFirstYahoo – A viral movement where celebs & normies confess their worst email names.

The Hero Film
The Yahoo Burner Mode Dinner Party – a TV ad that turns Yahoo email shame into a high-society nightmare.

IRL Activation
Yahoo Confessional Booths in major cities, where people admit their worst Yahoo emails.

Why This Works
Yahoo isn’t fighting how people use it—it’s leaning into it. It turns burner email behavior into something Yahoo OWNS.

Final Mic Drop Moment
Yahoo. With You for Life. Your first email. Your worst email. Your forever email.
